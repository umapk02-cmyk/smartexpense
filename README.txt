SMARTEXPENSE — DEPLOYMENT GUIDE
================================

STEP 1: Download this folder (smartexpense)
- It contains 2 files: index.html and app.js

STEP 2: Deploy to Netlify (FREE, takes 2 minutes)
1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Sign up with your Google account (free)
3. On the dashboard, find "Deploy manually" section
4. Drag and drop the entire "smartexpense" FOLDER onto the page
5. Netlify gives you a URL like: https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app
6. That's your permanent app URL!

STEP 3: Use from any device
- Bookmark the Netlify URL on your phone AND your husband's phone
- Both of you open the same URL
- Login with your PINs:
  Uma: 1111
  Husband: 2222
- All data syncs to your Google Sheet in real time

STEP 4: Rename your app (optional)
- In Netlify dashboard → Site settings → Change site name
- e.g. rename to: uma-homeexpense.netlify.app
